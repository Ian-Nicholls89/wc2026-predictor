How to Play
  1. Go to the Predict tab and enter your name.
  2. Fill in your scoreline predictions for all 72 World Cup matches.
  3. Hit Submit My Predictions before the tournament starts.
  4. You can edit any match prediction up until that match kicks off — once it's live, your score for it is locked.
  5. Use Lucky Dip to fill any empty scores at random.
